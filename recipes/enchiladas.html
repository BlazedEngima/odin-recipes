Chicken Enchiladas

Description

This chicken enchilada recipe is easy to make with tortillas, tender chicken, and
a deliciously cheesy sauce for a family-pleasing Mexican meal.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil as needed
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups of shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 pint sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon of dried parsely
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1/3 cup of chopped green bell peppers
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • Salt
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 8 (10 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 (12 ounce) jar taco sauce

Recipe Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C
  2. Heat olive oil in non-stick pan over medium heat. Add and cook the chicken
  3. Cut the chicken into cubes. Return the chicken to the skillet/pan
  4. Add onion, 1 cup of cheddar cheese, sour cream, parsley, oregano, and black pepper
  5. Cook and stir over low heat until cheese melts
  6. Add in tomato sauce, green pepper, garlic, chili powder, and salt. Add water if needed
  7. Spoon chicken mixture into tortillas and roll into enchiladas
  8. Top with taco sauce and the remainung cheddar cheese
  9. Bake in preheated oven for about 20 minutes
